Introduction

Bored of monotonous college assignments ?
Bring out the developer in you with Web Weaver 22 !

Web weaver is a web development event where the participants can participate individually or in a group of 2 and are required to submit websites on the given theme .Sites submitted on the first day are shortlisted and promoted for round 2 with specific changes to be applied.

Instructions

  • 1. Register Yourself for Web Weaver contest organised by PISB.
  • 2. Create a post to showup your website
  • 3. You have to upload Screen Shot of your website.
  • 4. In the information section of website, Name , Title, Link for your website
    and a short description about your website you have to submit.
  • 5. Viewers visits your website through the post that you have uploaded.
